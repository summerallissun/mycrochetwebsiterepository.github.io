How to Hold Your Hook and Yarn

Crocheting may take some time to get used to, but once you get the hang of it, it's actually very simple!

Holding your Hook:
There are two main ways to hold it and they are the knife hold or the pencil hold. For the knife hold, you would hold your hook like you would a knife, and for the pencil hold, hold it like it were a pencil.

Holding your Yarn:
Now how to hold your yarn. You want to hold your yarn in your left hand with the string going above your index finger. The rest of the string will be held loosely between your middle, ring, and pinkie fingers.
